v0.27
Summary
The main goals of this update were to bring different playable ships into the game, and to optimise my codebase with lessons I'd learned from my UE4 work. If I could accomplish that, working online multiplayer in down the line might become a real and exciting possibility. After 4 months of work, I've just about finished with it, on top of some extra improvements. After overhauling almost the entire codebase, every existing ship had their HP and movement mechanics standardised under the umbrella of 'BaseAsset', bringing parity between AI entities and the player ship in functionality. The new AI behaviour for FighterAssets took a much faster pace
System changes
  • AI controlled ships now work with the same mechanics as player ships, and vice versa
    • As a result, previously AI-only ships can now be player controlled. On top of the Altus, 3 more playable ships are now available
    • In some missions, you can take control of AI allies after death to carry on the fight
  • Fighter AI has been overhauled- I believe this new behaviour is more fun and realistic, but they can have a tendency to spin off into the distance when trying to take advantage of the stabiliser, and are generally place their shots much less accurately than before.
  • Separate physics mechanics have been added to captial ships to allow them to move around freely at last
  • A new mission ranking system has been introduced- push your skills to the limit to get an S-Rank in your favourite missions!
Main Menu
  • Descriptions for different buttons are displayed when hovering over them, letting you know what you're getting into before you press one
  • An options menu has been added where graphics quality presets can be selected, and different control presets can be opted into (Keyboard + Mouse, Gamepad + Keyboard, Flight Stick + Keyboard)

Altus F.2

REDFORS STRIKE FIGHTER

  • SGP Gunpods are now available for installation in the hangar, providing high velocity firepower with a serious wallop
    • They require some time to manually charge before firing, and once charged, they automatically fire after a fixed delay
    • Coming from this charge time are two lightning fast beams that deal 10x the damage of the Altus' integrated guns
    • Unlike HEP missiles, they draw from the Altus' replenishing store of weapon energy and do not require reloading trips for repeat firing
    • They can be fired in tandem with integrated weaponry, quickly cashing out on stored weapon energy for massive damage
  • Integrated Laser cannons have been further buffed to keep up with the increased pace of fighter on fighter combat
    • Muzzle velocity has been increased from 300->550ms^-1
  • HEP Missiles
    • A longtime bug causing HEP Missile starting velocity to be slower than the launching ship has been fixed
    • As a result, they boast quicker kill times and increased range
    • Explosion SFX have finally been added
    • The collider is extended when at very high speeds, to increase consistency of collisions
