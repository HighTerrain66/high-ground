v0.25
System changes
  • Mouse control is now available, functioning quite similarly to the flight controls in ArmA 3
  • A virtual soundscape has been added; you can now hear some synthesized sounds of enemy engines, gunfire, and missiles
  • All models have been scaled up by 1.6-2.215x to provide more realistic proportions to the metric system used by Unity
  • Damage, shield, mass and thrust values have been standardised across the board.
    • The player used to have 6 shields and 6 hull, going up against shots that did 1 damage
      On the other hand, enemy assets had within the realm of 40-200hp, going up against shots that did 12-24 damage
      Now the Altus has 150 shields and 150 hull, serving as a standard for other hp values to be based on
  • Collision damage is now properly based on alignment of velocity with collider normals- you come out much better from scraping an enemy transport at similar velocity than flying head first into an asteroid
  • Missile locking now has a visual indicator on the HUD- a white circle that closes around the target until it reaches minimum size and turns red (the lock tone is still in game, this indicator just makes things clearer)
  • All shot models have been replaced, also now featuring trails with consistent lengths
  • A shifting world origin has been introduced
    • Previously, when at positions of great magnitude(e.g. 5km away from world origin), objects would appear to oscillate due to
      floating point position imprecision, this should no longer occur
    • Because of this, maps introduced in future updates can be made larger
  • The render pipeline has been changed to URP- things look shinier now, and I generally prefer the look

Altus F.2

REDFORS STRIKE FIGHTER

  • Thrust to weight ratio has been buffed- there should be a noticeable increase in responsiveness
  • Laser cannons have received substantial buffs
    • They can now fire in full-auto
    • Muzzle velocity has been increased from 200->300ms^-1
    • The laser cannons now automatically adjust their facing to converge at the range the target is at, allowing for more consistent damage output
    • Capacitor energy has been increased, with max capacity at maintenance level being increased from 500->600, and the capacity at emergency power being increased from 1000->1300. This means you can fire off more shots in succession, especially with power diverted to weapons
  • Model changes
    • The model is now symmetrical, boasts greater detail, has larger laser cannons, and is shaded smoothly. The shading change might be reverted since the old look did have a certain charm to it
    • Added a targeting camera to the cockpit/HUD, alongside data about the target including 'health' values, target type, and relative velocity
    • Canopy superstructure has been added which houses a rearview mirror with a PIP display
    • Added a new shield display sprite (no more legacy sprite showing gunpods even if they aren't mounted)
  • HEP Missiles can now be dumbfired by pressing DELETE without a lock- this isn't realistic, but they do more damage when fired this way
  • A searchlight has been added which can be toggled with ;
    • It's handy for navigating the dark areas found inside asteroids and throughout Course 1 and Mission 2
